## Job Requirements Analysis

This document summarizes the common requirements for various technical and management roles, based on an analysis of recent job postings.

### Tech Lead

**Common Responsibilities and Skills:**

*   **Leadership and Strategy:**
    *   Guiding strategy and roadmap for AI capabilities.
    *   Leading engineering teams or cross-functional technical programs.
    *   Defining and maintaining technical vision and roadmap.
    *   Coming up with solutions for business projects and technical consultation.
    *   Mentoring peers and advocating for best practices.
*   **Technical Expertise:**
    *   **Programming Languages:** Python, TypeScript, Java, Go, .NET.
    *   **Cloud Platforms:** AWS, GCP, Azure.
    *   **Architecture:** API integration, microservices, enterprise systems architecture.
    *   **Specialized Areas:**
        *   Embedded Linux.
        *   Dataplane Engineering, packet processing, hardware acceleration (for networking-focused roles).
        *   Cloud-native environments (e.g., Cilium).
*   **Software Development:**
    *   Designing and developing secure software solutions.
    *   Leading design discussions.
    *   Enhancing technology products.
*   **Methodologies:**
    *   Agile methodologies.

### Manager (Technology)

**Common Responsibilities and Skills:**

*   **Strategic & Business Acumen:**
    *   Aligning IT systems and infrastructure with business goals.
    *   Steering the organization's technological direction.
    *   Managing client relationships.
    *   Developing IT policies.
    *   Technology licensing and portfolio management.
*   **Leadership & Team Management:**
    *   Leading teams of IT professionals.
    *   Overseeing technical teams.
    *   Strong leadership skills.
*   **Project & Program Management:**
    *   Managing projects and implementing new technologies.
    *   Delivering technology programs.
*   **Communication & Collaboration:**
    *   Strong communication skills.
    *   Collaboration with various stakeholders.

### Associate Manager (Technology)

**Common Responsibilities and Skills:**

*   **Team & Performance Management:**
    *   Oversee employees and improve productivity and performance.
    *   Provide guidance, mentorship, and support to team members.
    *   Delegate tasks, manage schedules, and ensure teams meet goals and deadlines.
*   **Technical & Domain-Specific:**
    *   (Depending on the role) Experience in Data Management, AI/LLM, Quality Operations, or Sales Strategy.
*   **Project & Operations Management:**
    *   Project planning, execution, and problem-solving.
    *   Monitoring budgets.
*   **General:**
    *   Strong interpersonal, communication, and leadership skills.
    *   Typically requires a bachelor's degree and some prior experience.

### AI Engineer

**Common Responsibilities and Skills:**

*   **Core AI/ML:**
    *   Deep understanding of machine learning and deep learning.
    *   Developing applications and systems using AI and machine learning techniques.
*   **Specializations:**
    *   Computer Vision, Reinforcement Learning, Robotics (depending on the role).
    *   Responsible AI.
*   **Software Engineering:**
    *   Strong software engineering skills.
    *   Python is a common language requirement.
*   **Tools & Platforms:**
    *   Experience with platforms like Vertex AI.
    *   MLOps.

### GenAI Engineer

**Common Responsibilities and Skills:**

*   **Core Generative AI:**
    *   Expertise in Generative AI concepts, including GANs, Diffusion models, Transformers, Autoencoders, and Large Language Models (LLMs).
    *   Experience with Agentic AI, NLP, and tools like Llama Index and Langchain.
    *   Developing and training generative AI models for various modalities (text, image, video).
*   **Technical Proficiency:**
    *   **Programming Languages:** Python.
    *   **ML Frameworks:** TensorFlow and PyTorch.
    *   **Cloud Platforms:** AWS, Azure, GCP.
    *   **Data:** Data cleaning, preparation, and pipeline creation.
    *   **MLOps:** Familiarity with MLOps practices.
    *   **Knowledge Graphs:** (For specific roles) RDF, OWL, SHACL, SPARQL, neo4j.
*   **Experience & Education:**
    *   Typically 3+ years of relevant experience as an AI Developer, with at least 2+ years specifically in Generative AI, LLMs, or Agentic AI.
    *   A Bachelor's, Master's, or Ph.D. degree in Engineering, Machine Learning, Computer Science, or a related quantitative field.
*   **Soft Skills:**
    *   Strong communication skills.
    *   Ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams.
    *   Interest in ethical AI, fairness, and bias in generative AI systems.
